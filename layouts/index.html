Our Platforms and Goals

Virtual Engagement. For Everyone. Anywhere.

We embrace the Internet as the primary method of engagement with our members and the entire engineering community.

Building the next generation workforce

Manufacturing is moving fast — and evolving. It can be challenging to stay on top of it all. We produce and launch multi-day workshops, snapshot webinars and other online educational events to help.

  • Join our Meetup Group

Open Source is our DNA

It may surprise you to learn that much of Smart Manufacturing is built on open source software and hardware. We embrace open source completely to provide hands-on educational opportunities and to encourage students and professionals to get engaged with open source.

  • View our GitHub projects
  • View this website's source code

Engagement on your schedule

Your time is at a premium nowadays. Busy work schedules. Traffic. Family life. We understand. All of our events are live-streamed, recorded and instantly available. We also build unique, engaging and immersive web experiences to enrich and educate.

  • Subcribe to our YouTube Channel

Conversations that are always on

Need some ideas for a class project? Want to ask the community for technical advice? Have any questions about a workshop? We are listening! Start a conversation today on any of the platforms we support. We welcome the public to participate in the conversations as well.

  • Join our Discord Chat Server
  • Join our Subreddit

Open By Design

A community is only as good as its ability to listen and adapt to the needs of its members. We welcome your feedback — good or bad. More than that, we strive to make our community entirely transparent in terms of finances, leadership meetings and how we impacting the community.

  • Follow us on Twitter
