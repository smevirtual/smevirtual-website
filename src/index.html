Mission

Our mission is to energize, mobilize and engage the global engineering and manufacturing community by bringing together industry, researchers, professionals and students to solve shared problems and relentlessly work to shrink the skills gap.

The SME Virtual Network is completely volunteer-powered.

Engagement Platforms

We embrace the accessibility and connectivity of the Internet to launch immersive virtual events, unique learning experiences and always-on conversations.

Our contributors have the option to use any or all of these platforms in unique ways to provide engagement opportunities to students and professionals.

Live-streamed and pre-recorded webinars, workshops and walkthroughs.

Our Principles

1

We are an open and welcoming community as we believe that diversity and inclusivity makes the industry better.

2

We emphasize open-source software, hardware, standards and content. Openness not empowers everyone equally — it is a necessary force to move the manufacturing industry forward.

3

We are fully transparent and entirely feedback-driven as we believe this is the only way to build the best possible engagement and collaboration platform.

4

We believe in direct action to challenge the skills gap. Actions are always stronger than words.
